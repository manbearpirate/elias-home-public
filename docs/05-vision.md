# Vision

Elias started as a small household helper.

But the long-term idea is broader.

The goal is to build a **calm household operating system** that helps support daily life without becoming intrusive or complicated.

Possible future areas include:

- household dashboards
- reminders and routines
- family-friendly voice queries
- meal planning support
- inventory awareness
- homestead record keeping
- garden and livestock logs

The key philosophy remains simple:

Technology should support the household, not dominate it.

Elias should feel like a **digital hearth**, not a command center.
